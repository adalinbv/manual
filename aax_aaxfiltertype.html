Filter Types

Filters and effects are applied in the following order:
  1. Occlusion
  2. Pitch related effects
  3. BitCrusher filter
  4. Modulator effect
  5. Frequency filter
  6. Distortion
  7. Delay effects (phasing/chorus/flanging)
  8. 1st order reverb
  9. Gain related filters
  10. Equalizer/2nd order Reverb
