enum aaxFrequencyFilterType

AAX_CONSTANT_VALUE (or true)
AAX_CONSTANT_VALUE or true equals to AAX_12DB_OCT
AAX_BUTTERWORTH
Butterworth filters are used in situations where a maximally flat magnitude is required and a maximally flat group/phase delay is of lesser importance. Usually an audio frequency filter is a Butterworth filter.
AAX_BESSEL
Bessel filters are used in situations where a maximally flat group/phase delay is required and a maximally flat magnitude is of lesser importance. Crossover filters are an example where this is required.
