Class Emitter

Header file: <aax/aeonwave>
Namespace: aeonwave

 

The Emitter class encapsulates the Emitter functions.

  • In stereo playback mode an emitter is just one of the sound sources that will be mixed with the other emitters at the requested pitch and volume.
  • In 3d virtual-space sound emitters are the virtual representation of sound sources. They can move around independently or relative to the sensor and have position, orientation en velocity properties. The sound they produce is defined in one or more attached audio buffers.
Filters and Effects can be applied to individual emitters to alter the way they sound.
