Class dsp

Header file: <aax/aeonwave.hpp>
Namespace: aax

 

The dsp class encapsulates Filter and Effect functions.

Audio filters provide a way to remove information of a particular sound.
Audio effects provide a way to change the characteristics of a particular sound.
