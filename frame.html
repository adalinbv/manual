Class Frame

Header file: <aax/aeonwave.hpp>
Namespace: aax

 

The Frame class encapsulates the AudioFrame functions.

Audio-frames serve three purposes:

  • Applying filters and effects to multiple stereo channels.
  • Repositioning and reorientation of a group of interconnected emitters and/or audio-frames with a single update call.
  • Serve as an obstruction or a cavity for indoor sound propagation.
