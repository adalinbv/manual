Parametric Equalizer

The Parametric Equalizer uses three user defined cut-off frequencies with adjustable resonance factor and four user definable frequency spectrum gain values resulting in a four band, fully user definable frequency filter.

If the value of the HF Cutoff Frequency parameter is below the value of the Cutoff Frequency parameter then both filters will be swapped.

The gain for the band-1 frequencies is the average of the HF Gain (p3) parameter of Slot-0 and the LF Gain HF (p2) parameter of Slot-1.
The gain for the band-2 frequencies is the average of the HF Gain (p3) parameter of Slot-1 and the LF Gain HF (p2) parameter of Slot-2.

Gain values up to 10.0 (+20dB) are allowed but may result in undesirable sound artefacts;
A high gain value might cause the audio compressor to take action but a too high value might result in audio clipping which produces an unpleasant form of audio distortion.

The Parametric Equalizer is supported by the Mixer.
The Parametric Equalizer and the Graphic Equalizer are mutually exclusive which means that only of of the two is supported at the same time.
The Parametric Equalizer is also supported by Audio-Frames for AeonWave version 2.6 and later.
Parametric Equalizer parameters
The filter uses two filter slots.
AAX_EQUALIZER
STEREO
Slot 0
p0
AAX_CUTOFF_FREQUENCY
Lower cut-off frequency in Hz.
p1
AAX_LF_GAIN
Gain of all frequencies below the cut-off frequency
p2
AAX_HF_GAIN
Gain of all frequencies between the lower and higher cut-off frequency
p3
AAX_RESONANCE
Resonance factor of the frequency filter.
Between 1.0 en 100.0, defaults to 1.0
Slot 1
p0
AAX_CUTOFF_FREQUENCY_HF
Higher cut-off frequency in Hz.
p1
AAX_LF_GAIN_HF
Gain of all frequencies between the lower and higher cut-off frequency
p2
AAX_HF_GAIN_HF
Gain of all frequencies greater than the cut-off frequency
p3
AAX_RESONANCE_HF
Resonance factor of the frequency filter.
Between 1.0 en 100.0, defaults to 1.0
Slot 2
p0
AAX_CUTOFF_FREQUENCY_HF
Higher cut-off frequency in Hz.
p1
AAX_LF_GAIN_HF
Gain of all frequencies between the lower and higher cut-off frequency
p2
AAX_HF_GAIN_HF
Gain of all frequencies greater than the cut-off frequency
p3
AAX_RESONANCE_HF
Resonance factor of the frequency filter.
Between 1.0 en 100.0, defaults to 1.0
Parametric Equalizer State options
Specify true to enable the parametric equalizer or false to disable it.
